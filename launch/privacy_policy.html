Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

At Tspace Portfolio, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors and app users. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Tspace Portfolio and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

When you register and use Tspace Portfolio, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Your name, email, profile picture, and account credentials (such as issued Sanctum authentication tokens).
  • OAuth Authentication Data: When logging in via third-party providers (such as GitHub), we receive and store authentication headers and user IDs needed to retrieve profile data.
  • Synced Integrations: If you select to sync public repositories, we collect metadata about those items (project names, descriptions, stars count, language types).
  • Uploaded Files: Profile photos, portfolio backgrounds, and PDF resumes uploaded to our asset storage server.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To build and serve your public portfolio page.
  • To maintain and authenticate your account sessions.
  • To process file uploads and store resumes/images.
  • To deliver messages sent by recruiters to your inbox.

3. Data Protection and Security

Your password is cryptographically hashed, and account session tokens are stored securely in local app storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore). We enforce secure HTTPS encryption for all client-to-server data transfers.

4. Your Data Deletion Rights

You can request full deletion of your account and all associated portfolio block data at any time by contacting our support team inside the settings panel.

5. Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@tspace.me.
