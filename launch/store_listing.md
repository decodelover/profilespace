## 📝 Long Description

**Showcase Your Work. Build Your Presence. Get Hired.**

Tspace Portfolio is the ultimate mobile website builder designed specifically for tech professionals, creators, writers, and developers. Craft a beautiful, interactive, and responsive portfolio website right from your device—no coding or hosting required.

Using our state-of-the-art **Bento Grid Layout**, you can showcase your project repositories, design links, YouTube channels, newsletter RSS feeds, and quick bio cards within minutes.

### 🌟 Key Features

* **Polymorphic Bento Grid Editor**: Arrange cards with dynamic layouts using our iOS-inspired "Wiggle & Move" reorder gestures.
* **Auto-Discovery Syncing**: Log in with GitHub to auto-import your top repositories, stars, and language badges directly as live tiles.
* **10+ Pre-built Card Styles**: Add blocks for Profiles, Figma embeds, RSS feeds, YouTube streams, monospace prompt collections, metric counters, custom links, and rich images.
* **Instant Deployments**: Publish your portfolio instantly to a fast, cached public URL (`tspace.me/yourname`).
* **Rich Glassmorphic Design**: Impress recruiters and collaborators at first glance with responsive glassmorphic cards and customized font families.
* **Secure Recruiter Inbox**: Allow hiring managers to send messages directly to your email inbox, clean and spam-protected.
